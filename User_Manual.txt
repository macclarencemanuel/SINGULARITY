SINGULARITY - USER MANUAL

1. Game Overview

SINGULARITY is a sci-fi horror shooter game where the player wakes up inside a mysterious space facility after their consciousness has been uploaded. The player must survive hostile entities, clear rooms, collect credits, upgrade equipment, defeat bosses, and escape after Room 10.

The game includes:
- Intro video
- Title screen
- Main menu
- How to Play screen
- Game intro cutscene
- Room-based combat
- Shop/terminal upgrade system
- Boss fights
- Ending sequence


2. How to Start the Game

1. Open the playable GitHub Pages link.
2. Wait for the intro sequence to finish.
3. Click the title screen or press any key.
4. Click Play.
5. Read the How to Play screen.
6. Click Start Game.
7. Watch or skip the game intro cutscene.
8. Begin playing.


3. Controls

Move:
W A S D

Run:
Hold Shift

Aim:
Mouse

Shoot:
Left Click

Pause:
Esc

Open Shop/Terminal:
Press T when prompted

Menu Navigation:
Use mouse clicks on the buttons.


4. Main Objective

The goal of the game is to progress through 10 rooms and escape the facility.

The player must:
1. Defeat all enemies in each room.
2. Collect loot and credits.
3. Open the shop/terminal when prompted.
4. Buy upgrades if enough credits are available.
5. Move to the next room.
6. Defeat the boss in Room 5.
7. Defeat the final boss in Room 10.
8. Escape and complete the ending sequence.


5. Room System

The game has a maximum of 10 rooms.

Room 1 starts easier.
Enemy difficulty gradually increases as the player progresses.
Room 5 contains the first boss.
Room 10 contains the final boss.
After Room 10, the player escapes and reaches the ending.


6. Enemy Types

Charger Enemy:
Moves toward the player and attacks at close range.

Long Distance Shooter Enemy:
Keeps distance from the player and shoots projectiles.

Boss Enemy:
Appears in Room 5 and Room 10.
Shoots a large projectile.
Has higher health and damage than normal enemies.


7. Loot System

Enemies may drop items after being defeated.

Credits:
Used to buy upgrades in the shop.

Heal:
Restores player health.


8. Shop / Terminal System

After clearing a room and collecting loot, the player can open the terminal shop by pressing T when prompted.

Available upgrades:

Space Boots Upgrade:
Increases player speed.

Space Gloves Upgrade:
Increases attack speed.

Space Suit Upgrade:
Adds shield.

Space Helmet Upgrade:
Increases maximum HP.

Repair:
Restores 1 HP.


9. Health and Game Over

The player has HP and shield.

If the player takes damage:
- Shield is removed first if available.
- HP is reduced if no shield is available.

If HP reaches 0:
- The game fades to the Game Over screen.
- The screen displays room reached, credits collected, enemies defeated, and time survived.
- The player can restart or quit to the menu.


10. Ending

After defeating the final boss in Room 10, the exit changes to Escape.
When the player escapes:
1. A fade effect plays.
2. A humorous ending screen appears.
3. A celebration sound plays.
4. A serious completion screen appears.
5. The player returns to the main menu.
